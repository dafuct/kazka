## Frequently asked questions

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Depends where you signed up:

- **Apple App Store (iPhone/iPad)** — Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Kazka → Cancel.
- **Monobank Acquiring (Ukrainian card)** — open Settings inside Kazka and turn off auto-renewal. Your Pro access stays until the end of the period you already paid for.

### How do I delete my account?

Email `d.makarenko.home@gmail.com` from the address tied to the account. We'll delete the account and all your tales within 30 days.

### Why was my tale blocked / my account suspended?

The service has automatic moderation that blocks requests for violent, sexual, or otherwise child-harmful content. If you think it was a mistake, email us — we'll review.

### The tale came out weird — invented words, or didn't make sense

That's AI being AI — it sometimes "hallucinates". You can regenerate for free with the "Create again" button on the tale's detail page. If it happens often, send us an example you didn't like — that helps us improve the models.

### I'm not getting the verification email

- Check spam / filtered folders.
- Try again after 5 minutes — there's a "Resend" button on the sign-in page.
- If it never arrives, email `d.makarenko.home@gmail.com` with the address you used and we'll verify manually.

### Can I use Kazka in a school / classroom?

Yes — but write to us first. We can offer special terms for educational settings (group accounts, distinct licensing).

### How do I add another child?

In the app: Settings → Child Profiles → Add. The free tier supports one profile; Pro is unlimited.

### How do I redeem a gift code?

Open the app → menu → "Redeem code" → enter the code from your gift card. Once activated, Pro turns on for the duration printed on the code.

## Reporting tale errors

If a tale comes out with invented words, broken plot, or just feels weak — send us a short example. We collect cases like this to train the models better.

## Reporting abuse

If you see something that endangers children, or believe the service is being misused, email `d.makarenko.home@gmail.com` with "abuse" in the subject. We treat these as top priority.
